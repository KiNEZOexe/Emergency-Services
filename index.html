Find the nearest Emergency Services

City, Province (Ex: San Fernando, Pampanga)

    Process:

    1. STEP 1: User entered a location.
    2. STEP 2: The system converted the location into coordinates using Geocoding.
    3. STEP 3: Nearby hospitals, fire stations, and police stations were fetched from OpenStreetMap.
    4. STEP 4: Distances between the user and each emergency service were calculated using the Haversine Formula.
    5. STEP 5: The Greedy Algorithm compared all calculated distances.
    6. STEP 6: The Greedy Algorithm prioritized the nearest emergency services first.
    7. STEP 7: The system sorted the emergency services from nearest to farthest.
    8. STEP 8: The top 10 closest emergency services were selected.
    9. STEP 9: The selected emergency services were displayed to the user.
